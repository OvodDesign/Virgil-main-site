Who is Virgil Abloh?

Virgil Abloh was a visionary designer, artist, and entrepreneur who transformed the worlds of fashion, art, and culture.

As the founder of the iconic streetwear brand Off-White and the first Black artistic director of Louis Vuitton's menswear division

Abloh redefined the boundaries between luxury fashion and street style.

His work was known for its deconstructionist approach, where he blurred the lines between art, fashion, and architecture, creating designs that challenged convention and celebrated creativity in its purest form.
